Homemade Pizza

Description

Take a trip to Italy in your own kitchen with this delcious pizza recipe!

Ingredients

  • Mozzerarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Dough

Steps

  1. Spread out the dough into a circular shape
  2. Add Tomato sauce to the top of the dough
  3. Add cheese and any other toppings you would like
  4. Bake until ready
