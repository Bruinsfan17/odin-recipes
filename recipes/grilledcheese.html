3 Cheese Grilled Cheese

Description

This delicious sandwich will bring you back to your childhood, featuring three different cheeses!

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Butter
  • American Cheese
  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Smoked Gouda Cheese

Steps

  • Butter two slices of bread
  • Place one slice of each type of cheese inbetween the pieces of bread
  • Toast the sandwhich in a pan until each side is browned and the cheese is melted
