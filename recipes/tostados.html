Tostado

Description

This delicious Tex-Mex meal has everything you could want, including cheesy and crisp tortillas, spicy ground beef, and yummy vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Roma Tomato
  • Lime
  • Yellow Onion
  • Hot Sauce
  • Southwest Spice Blend
  • Beef Stock Conncentrate
  • Mexian Cheese Blend
  • Cilantro
  • Long Green Pepper
  • Sour Cream
  • Ground Beef
  • Chili Powder
  • Flour Tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees and chop up vegetables.
  2. Heat a small drizzle of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add ground beef and Southwest spice.
  3. Once beef is browned, add veggies and beef concentrate. Cook until veggies are softened.
  4. Rub each tortilla with oil and prick them with a fork. Bake on the top rack until browned. 4 minutes on each side.
  5. Add cheese to tortillas and return to oven until melted. Remove from oven and add beef and veggies.
  6. Enjoy!
